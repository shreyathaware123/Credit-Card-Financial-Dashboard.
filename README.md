## A) Credit Card  Transaction Dashboard

* Overall Performance: Revenue is 55M, Amount is 45M, Interest is 8M, and Count is 26M.
* Card Category: Blue card dominates with 46M revenue, significantly higher than Silver (6M).
* Quarterly Trend: Revenue steadily declines from Q1 (15M) to Q4 (12.2M).
* Transaction Method: Swipe generates the highest revenue (35M), followed by Chip (17M).
* Spending Type: Bills is the top expense type (14M), followed by Entertainment (10M).
* Customer Job: Businessman customers generate the most revenue (17M).
* Customer Education: Graduate customers account for the highest revenue at 22M.

  ## A) Credit Card  Customer Dashboard


* **Overall Performance:** Revenue is **55M**, Amount is **45M**, Income is **576M**, and Customer Satisfaction Score (CSS) is **3.19**.
* **Gender:** **Males (M)** generate higher revenue (**30M**) than **Females (F)** (**25M**).
* **Transaction Method:** **Swipe** transactions are the highest source of revenue (**35M**), followed by **Chip (17M)** and **Online (3M)**.
* **Quarterly Trend:** Revenue is highest in **Q1** and lowest in **Q4** (similar to the Transaction Report).
* **Age Group:** The **40-50** age group generates the most revenue (**14M**), followed by **50-60 (10M)**.
* **Customer Job:** **Businessman** customers lead in revenue (**17M**), income (**187M**), and interest earned (**2.539K**).
* **Top States:** **TX** and **NY** are the top revenue-generating states, both at **7M**.
* **Marital Status:** **Married** customers contribute the highest revenue (**15M**), with **13M** from Blue cards.
* **Income Group:** The **High** income group generates the most revenue (**22M**), followed by the **Low** group (**10M**).
* **Education Level:** **Graduate** customers bring in the most revenue (**12M**), followed by **High School (6M)**.
* **Dependent Count:** Customers with **2** dependents are the most valuable, generating **8M** in revenue.
